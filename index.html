About Me

'Hello World!' My name is Fernando Zacarias and I am a certified Web Developer from the University of Arizona Full Stack Coding Bootcamp.

This is my story. While working in accounting, I developed a growing interest in computer applications and technology that has since turned into a full-blown passion. After a few months of research and self-teaching, I decided to take the plunge and immerse myself in programming. As a person whose career has been focused on accuracy, data, and numbers, I strive to couple my newfound software skillset with that approach to productivity and quality.

The specific software development skills in my toolbelt include: JavaScript, Node.js, Express, React.js, jQuery, HTML5/CSS3, Bootstrap, Materialize, Git, Responsive Design, API/JSON, RESTful API, Firebase, MySQL, MongoDB, AJAX, and Database Modeling.

In addition to full stack web development skills, I have working experience using Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD, Postman, Terminal, QuickBooks, Windows and Mac OS's, and the complete MS Office Suite. Please check out some of my projects in the portfolio section below! Note that I will continue to add new passion projects as they are completed, so please be sure to come back in the future to see how my portfolio progresses!
